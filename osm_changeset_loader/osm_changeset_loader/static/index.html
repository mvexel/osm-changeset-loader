Meet Your Mappers!

Find out who has been mapping in your (or any) area!

Instructions

  • Go to OSM and center the map on the area you are interested in
  • Copy the URL (https://www.openstreetmap.org/#map=...)
  • Paste the URL in the form on this page
  • Select the scale of the region you are interested in
  • Click on Meet My Mappers
  • After about 10 seconds, you will see a table with active mappers in your area!
